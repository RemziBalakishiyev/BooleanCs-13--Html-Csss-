Albert Einstein was born in Ulm,[7] in the Kingdom of Württemberg in the German Empire , on 14 March 1879 a family of secular Ashkenazi Jews.[21][22] His parents were Hermann Einstein, a salesman and engineer , and Pauline Koch. In 1880, the family moved to Munich, where Einstein's father and his uncle Jakob founded Elektrotechnische Fabrik J. Einstein & Cie, a company that manufactured electrical equipment based on direct current.[7] Albert attended a Catholic elementary school in Munich, from the age of five, for three years.
